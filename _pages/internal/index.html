Ideally, this is useful information about working in EVL and for Dr. Papka at UIC.

If you are new to the lab, please fill out the form here - this provides me with important information that I will use throughout your time at UIC and historical data on all the students I have worked with and when.

If I am funding you, you have likely met Dana Plepys (dplepys@uic.edu), but if not, you need to meet her - she will help get you on the payroll, request a key for you to EVL spaces, and get you added to appropriate email lists. After Dana, you want to make sure you meet Lance Long (llong4@uic.edu), and he will get you access to EVL resources as needed and provide an overview of the EVL space. Please join the EVL Slack, particularly the papka-lab (ask to be added) channel.
