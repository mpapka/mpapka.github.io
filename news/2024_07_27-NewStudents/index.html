Excited for the arrival of seven new students this year!

July 27, 2024

2024

Happy to announce the arrival of seven new students this year! With Professor Andy Johnson’s retirement, I am pleased to now co-advise Andrew Burks and Shreyas Kulkarni in collaboration with Andy. We also welcome three new PhD students: Yash Kurkure, Jyotsna Rajaraman, and Chris Grams, whom I will be co-advising with Professor Yu Gao. Additionally, we have two new MS students, Amy Byrnes and Wesley Kwiecinski, both joining as \(C^2\) the \(P^2\) Fellows.
